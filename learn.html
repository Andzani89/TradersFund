Trading Glossary

Liquidity

How easily an asset can be bought or sold without affecting its price.

Spread

The difference between the buy and sell price of an asset.

Leverage

Using borrowed capital to increase potential returns.

FOMO

"Fear Of Missing Out" - emotional trading to avoid missing price moves.

Stop-Loss

An order to automatically sell at a certain price to limit losses.

Whale

An individual or entity with enough capital to move market prices.

ATH

"All Time High" - the highest price an asset has ever reached.

DYOR

"Do Your Own Research" - advice to verify information yourself.
