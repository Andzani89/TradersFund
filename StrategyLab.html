Core Trading Strategy Types

Understand the fundamental approaches to market speculation

→ Trend Following

Capitalizes on sustained price movements in one direction. Works best in strongly trending markets.

  • Buy highs and sell higher in uptrends
  • Sell lows and buy lower in downtrends
  • Uses indicators like moving averages, ADX, MACD
  • Requires patience through pullbacks

↔ Mean Reversion

Based on the concept that prices tend to return to their average over time. Works best in ranging markets.

  • Sell overbought conditions, buy oversold
  • Uses indicators like RSI, Bollinger Bands
  • Requires identifying range boundaries
  • Higher win rate but smaller profits

↑ Breakout Trading

Seeks to capitalize on price movements beyond defined support/resistance levels.

  • Enter when price breaks key levels with volume
  • False breakouts are major risk
  • Works well after consolidation periods
  • Often uses chart patterns like triangles

🚀 Momentum Trading

Seeks to capture gains by riding existing market momentum in the short-term.

  • Buy assets showing upward acceleration
  • Short assets with downward acceleration
  • Uses volume and price velocity indicators
  • Requires quick entry/exit execution

⇄ Arbitrage

Exploits price differences of the same asset across different markets or forms.

  • Requires fast execution and low fees
  • Common in crypto (cross-exchange arb)
  • Includes statistical arbitrage strategies
  • Low risk but opportunities are fleeting

📰 News Trading

Capitalizes on market volatility around news events and economic releases.

  • Requires fastest news feed access
  • Can be automated with NLP algorithms
  • High risk due to whipsaw potential
  • Includes "buy the rumor, sell the news"

Timeframe Strategies

Different approaches for various holding periods

Scalping

Seconds to Minutes

Ultra-short-term trades aiming for small, frequent profits. Requires intense focus and low commissions.

  • 5-20 trades per day
  • 1:1 or better risk-reward
  • 90%+ win rate target
  • Order flow analysis critical

Day Trading

Minutes to Hours

All positions closed before market close. Capitalizes on intraday volatility and patterns.

  • 1-5 trades per day
  • 1.5:1 or better risk-reward
  • 60-70% win rate target
  • Technical analysis focused

Swing Trading

Days to Weeks

Captures short-to-medium term trends. Balances frequency with profit potential.

  • 5-10 trades per month
  • 2:1 or better risk-reward
  • 50-60% win rate target
  • Combines technical and fundamental

Position Trading

Weeks to Years

Long-term approach based on macroeconomic trends and fundamentals.

  • 5-10 trades per year
  • 3:1 or better risk-reward
  • 40-50% win rate target
  • Fundamental analysis focused
