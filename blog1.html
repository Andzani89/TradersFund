Best Strategies for Trading with Bonus Funds

To make the most of your no-deposit bonus, consider these proven strategies:

1. Start Small and Scale Up

Begin with small position sizes (0.01 lots for forex) to preserve your capital while you get comfortable with the platform and market conditions.

2. Focus on High-Probability Trades

Only take trades with strong technical setups and favorable risk/reward ratios (at least 1:2).

3. Use Stop-Loss Orders Religiously

Protect your bonus funds by always using stop-loss orders to limit potential losses.

4. Compound Your Profits

As your account grows, gradually increase position sizes while maintaining the same risk percentage per trade.

Common Bonus Terms to Watch For

Before claiming any no-deposit bonus, carefully review these common terms:

  • Trading volume requirements: How much you need to trade before withdrawing
  • Time limits: How long the bonus remains active
  • Withdrawal restrictions: Limits on how much profit you can withdraw
  • Eligible instruments: Which assets you can trade with the bonus

Common Mistakes to Avoid

Many beginners make these mistakes when trading with no-deposit bonuses:

  • Overtrading to meet volume requirements
  • Taking excessive risks with bonus funds
  • Ignoring the bonus terms and conditions
  • Not verifying their account before trading
